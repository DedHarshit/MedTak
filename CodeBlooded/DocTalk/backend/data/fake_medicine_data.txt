Paracetamol:
Used for fever and mild pain.
Side effects: nausea, rash.

Ibuprofen:
Used for inflammation and pain relief.
Side effects: stomach irritation.

Ibuprofen and Aspirin Interaction:
Taking ibuprofen with aspirin may reduce aspirin's heart protection effect.

Amoxicillin:
Antibiotic used for bacterial infections.
Side effects: diarrhea, nausea.
